BeLS is running professional development workshops covering:

  • Using Moodle - to support your learners with unit outlines, resources, activities, and assessments
  • Using Mahara e-Portfolios - so that your students can store, discuss, and present their work and experiences online
  • Mobility in education - providing many ways for your students to create and access content - using iPads, iPhones, and Android devices
  • Social collaboration - building online communities to support your learners and learning
  • Video collaboration with Echo360 - creating video recordings of classes, demonstrations, etc, and making them available for online discussion

Click on the workshops for details. Book for a session by clicking 'Register' on this page - not by sending emails. Please unregister if you later find out you can't come, so that we know who is coming.

For enquiries, email Ian Goding at i.goding@boxhill.edu.au

More workshops and details will be added from time to time...

Register - don't just turn up. We won't run the workshop if there are not enough people, and we can only notify people who are registered of cancellations.
